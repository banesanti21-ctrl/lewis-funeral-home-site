About Us

The caring memorial service directors at Lewis Funeral Home provide customized funeral services designed to meet the needs of every family. The professional, devoted staff can help you in making memorial service arrangements, funeral arranging, and assist you through cremation options. The burial director can guide you through all facets of the program; including burial flowers, casket choices, suitable music selection, and local accommodations. Trust is their top concern and so they specialize in tradition funerals, basic cremation, grief support, urn selection, and military funeral service services including offering veteran burial flags.
